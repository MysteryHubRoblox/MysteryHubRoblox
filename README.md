<h2>Our Pledge</h2>

<p>
As members, contributors, and maintainers of <strong>Mystery Hub</strong>, we pledge to make participation in this project a harassment-free and respectful experience for everyone.
</p>

<p>
<strong>Mystery Hub is a script designed for Roblox games.</strong> This community exists to support the development, improvement, and responsible use of the script within the Roblox environment.
</p>

<p>
We are committed to maintaining an open, inclusive, and healthy environment for all users and developers.
</p>

<hr>

<h2>Our Standards</h2>

<h3>Positive behavior includes</h3>
<ul>
  <li>Respecting other users and script developers</li>
  <li>Sharing ideas, improvements, and bug reports constructively</li>
  <li>Helping others understand how to use Mystery Hub in Roblox games</li>
  <li>Keeping discussions focused on code, features, and performance</li>
  <li>Accepting constructive criticism and learning from it</li>
  <li>Acting in the best interest of the community and the project</li>
</ul>

<h3>Unacceptable behavior includes</h3>
<ul>
  <li>Insults, harassment, mockery, or personal attacks</li>
  <li>Trolling or intentionally causing conflicts</li>
  <li>Sexual, offensive, or inappropriate language</li>
  <li>Sharing private information without permission</li>
  <li>Sabotaging the script or intentionally harming the project</li>
  <li>Any behavior that is not appropriate for a development-focused environment</li>
</ul>

<hr>

<h2>Enforcement Responsibilities</h2>

<p>
The maintainers of <strong>Mystery Hub</strong> are responsible for enforcing this Code of Conduct.
They have the right to remove, edit, or reject comments, commits, issues, pull requests, or other contributions that do not comply with these standards.
</p>

<hr>

<h2>Scope</h2>

<p>
This Code of Conduct applies to all official spaces related to <strong>Mystery Hub</strong>, including:
</p>

<ul>
  <li>GitHub repositories</li>
  <li>Issues and Pull Requests</li>
  <li>Discord servers or other official communities</li>
  <li>Any space where Mystery Hub is discussed or officially represented</li>
</ul>

<hr>

<h2>Enforcement Guidelines</h2>

<p><strong>1. Correction</strong><br>
Impact: Inappropriate language or unprofessional behavior<br>
Consequence: Private written warning explaining the issue
</p>

<p><strong>2. Warning</strong><br>
Impact: Repeated behavior or a more serious violation<br>
Consequence: Formal warning and temporary restriction from participation
</p>

<p><strong>3. Temporary Suspension</strong><br>
Impact: Serious or repeated misconduct<br>
Consequence: Temporary suspension from Mystery Hub community spaces
</p>

<p><strong>4. Permanent Ban</strong><br>
Impact: Harassment, abuse, or intentional harm to the project or community<br>
Consequence: Permanent removal from all official Mystery Hub spaces
</p>
